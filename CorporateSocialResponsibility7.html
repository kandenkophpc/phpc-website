"Dugo Mula Sa Puso" Blood Donation Drive at Metro Lipa Medical Center

PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc. — KANDENKO Group

PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc., a proud member of the KANDENKO Group based in Makati City, successfully hosted its Corporate Social Responsibility initiative, PHPCares, with a heartfelt blood donation drive themed "DUGO MULA SA PUSO" (Blood from the Heart).

"Dugo Mula Sa Puso" — Blood from the Heart. This initiative underscores PHPC's commitment to giving back to the community and saving lives through voluntary blood donation. Every drop counts, and every donor is a hero."

The event was organized in cooperation with Metro Lipa Medical Center (MLMC) and in partnership with the Philippine National Red Cross, Lipa City. Held on June 8, 2024 at the Metro Lipa Medical Center, this lifesaving initiative brought together PHPC employees, partners, and members of the local community in a united effort to donate blood and support those in need.

🩸 The Importance of Blood Donation

Blood donation is one of the most direct and impactful ways to save lives. Every blood donation can help up to three patients in need — whether for emergency surgeries, cancer treatments, childbirth complications, or chronic illnesses. In the Philippines, the demand for safe blood is constant, and voluntary blood drives like PHPCares play a crucial role in maintaining the national blood supply.

🤝 A Community United for a Cause

The "Dugo Mula Sa Puso" blood drive exemplified the power of community partnership. With Metro Lipa Medical Center providing the venue and medical expertise, and the Philippine Red Cross ensuring proper blood collection and storage protocols, PHPC facilitated an event that was both safe and impactful. Volunteers and employees lined up throughout the day, demonstrating that the spirit of bayanihan (community unity) is alive and well.

💙 PHPCares: More Than Just a Program

PHPCares represents the company's ongoing commitment to corporate social responsibility that goes beyond business operations. From educational support to environmental initiatives, and now to health and wellness programs, PHPC continues to expand its reach and impact across Filipino communities. The "Dugo Mula Sa Puso" blood drive is a shining example of how companies can leverage their resources and networks to make a tangible difference in people's lives.

Our company extends its deepest gratitude to every donor, volunteer, and partner who made this event a success. Together, we proved that when we give from the heart, we can help save lives and strengthen our communities. PHPC remains committed to continuing programs like this, knowing that each act of kindness, no matter how small, ripples outward to create lasting change.
