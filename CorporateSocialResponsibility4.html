CSR Activity June 10, 2021 4 min read

PHPC Corporate Social Responsibility Activity 4
Langkaan Elementary School, Langkaan 1, Dasmariñas, Cavite



Despite the pandemic we are facing today, which is caused by the Coronavirus (also known as COVID-19), the executives of PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc. planned and discussed agendas on how to execute supportive actions that would help in some ways our Filipino citizens — especially at this moment in time — as an essential part of our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).

"Our company aims to continue this kind of program and to widen our scope — not just by merely practicing our CSR, but by having the heart to continue serving and helping people in any way that we can."

The company communicated with the Department of Education (DepEd) to help us look for schools who most need our assistance and help. From Metro Manila, our team travelled to Langkaan 1, Dasmariñas, Cavite to visit Langkaan Elementary School, one of the beneficiaries of our growing CSR initiative.

As the fourth leg of PHPC's CSR outreach program, this visit to Langkaan Elementary School marked another meaningful step in our journey of giving back to Filipino communities. With each school we visit, we are reminded of the resilience and determination of our educators and students who continue to pursue learning despite the immense challenges brought by the pandemic. Understanding the critical role that technology plays in modern education, PHPC proudly donated 8 sets of computers to support the faculty and students of Langkaan Elementary School in their transition to blended and distance learning modalities.

💻 DONATION OF 8 SETS COMPUTER AT LANGKAAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

The smiles and heartfelt gratitude from the teachers, parents, and school administrators of Langkaan Elementary School affirmed the importance of our mission. Each computer set is more than just equipment — it is a bridge to opportunity, a tool for empowerment, and a symbol of hope for a brighter tomorrow. Guided by our core values of malasakit (compassion) and bayanihan (community spirit), PHPC continues to stand with Filipino communities in times of need.

Our company aims to continue this kind of program and to widen our scope — not just by merely practicing our CSR, but by having the heart to continue serving and helping people in any way that we can. With every school we reach, we move one step closer to a future where no Filipino child is left behind.
