PHPC Co., Ltd. Inc. Achieves 5 Million Safe Man-Hours



PHPC Co., Ltd., Inc. proudly announces the achievement of 5 million safe man-hours, a significant safety milestone that reflects the company's growing commitment to workplace safety and operational excellence.

"Five million safe man-hours is more than a number — it represents our unwavering promise to protect every worker. This milestone marks the foundation of a safety culture that continues to grow stronger each day."

This achievement of 5 million safe man-hours without a lost time incident demonstrates PHPC's growing safety management systems and the collective effort of every employee and contractor. Despite the challenges of the pandemic period, PHPC maintained its focus on safety through rigorous training, daily toolbox meetings, and continuous site monitoring.

🏗️ Building the Foundation of Safety Excellence:
• Comprehensive safety orientation and training for all personnel
• Regular safety audits and proactive workplace inspections
• Strict adherence to PPE requirements and safety protocols
• Proactive hazard identification and risk assessment
• Employee wellness and health monitoring programs
• Emergency response preparedness and drills

👷 A Milestone That Paved the Way

This milestone belongs to every worker who showed up each day committed to doing their job safely during one of the most challenging periods in recent history. It honors the safety officers who remained vigilant, the supervisors who led by example, and the management team that ensured safety remained the top priority even amidst a global pandemic.

As we celebrated this achievement, PHPC renewed its commitment to reaching even greater heights in workplace safety. From 5 million, we set our sights on the next milestones ahead, knowing that every safe man-hour represents a worker who returns home safely to their family at the end of each day.
